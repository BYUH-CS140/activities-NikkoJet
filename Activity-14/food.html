Pork Sinigang

          
                     

This hearty dish is my favorite comfort food. Here's what I use:

          
            

Ingredients:

            
                  
  • 500g pork belly (cut into chunks)
    •               
  • 500g American pork ribs (cut into individual ribs)
    •               
  • 2 medium onions (sliced)
    •               
  • 2.5cm knob ginger (smashed and chopped)
    •               
  • 2 tomatoes (quartered)
    •               
  • 150g snake beans (cut into 5cm lengths)
    •               
  • Chilies (green or red, optional)
    •               
  • 1 small eggplant (cut into 5cm lengths)
    •               
  • 6–8 okra
    •               
  • 200g kangkong (water spinach)
    •               
  • 2–2.5 liters water (adjust based on sourness preference)
    •               
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
    •               
  • 1 pack Sinigang tamarind soup mix
    •               
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
    •               
  • Salt to taste (optional)
    •             
          
          

It’s delicious, easy to cook, and perfect for family dinners!
