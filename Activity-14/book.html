About the Book:

                         

Summary

            
                  
  • "How to Hug a Porcupine" by Debbie Ellis is a self-help book using a porcupine metaphor for difficult relationships.
    •               
  • It teaches empathy, patience, and kindness by recognizing others' fears and needs.
    •               
  • It emphasizes setting boundaries and responding to defensiveness with care.
    •             
          
          
            

Ellis’s Tips for “Hugging” a Porcupine:

            
                  
  1. Know Their Warning Signs: Learn to recognize emotional triggers.
    2.               
  2. Keep Your Quills In: Stay calm and don’t respond defensively. (p.13)
    3.               
  3. Respect Boundaries: Be gentle and patient.
    4.               
  4. Understand Real Needs: Many reactions come from past trauma.
    5.               
  5. Find the Soft Spot: Connect by finding shared values.
