BOOK REVIEW: How to Hug a Porcupine:

Easy Ways to Love the Difficult People in Your Life

About the Book:

BS:

    "How to Hug a Porcupine" by Debbie Ellis is a self-help book that

      uses the analogy of a porcupine to illustrate how to deal with difficult people in your life.

    The book suggests that by understanding the "porcupine's" (i.e., the difficult person's) fears and motivations, you can approach them with patience, empathy, and kindness, rather than escalating conflict.

    The analogy encourages readers to recognize and respect the boundaries of these individuals, and to find ways to connect with their soft spots, ultimately promoting peaceful interactions.
