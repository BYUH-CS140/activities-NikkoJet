Aloha! My name is Nikko. I'm 23 years old and I'm from the Philippines.

The purpose of this website is to help you get to know more about me, where I came from, my favorite book, and how to make my favorite food.

I am a student here in Brigham Young University–Hawaii, majoring in Computer Science, and I’m taking Visual Arts and Entrepreneurship as my minors.

Growing up, art was one of the things I loved doing. I had a passion to become both an architect and engineer. In fact, I studied Architecture at the University of the Philippines–Diliman, one of the top universities in the Philippines.

When I got here, I pursued my love for Computer Science. Even though the majors are different, the purpose remains the same.
